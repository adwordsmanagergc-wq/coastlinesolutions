### Seeded Q&A (post these yourself so they appear instantly)

**Q: Do you offer free quotes?**
A: Yes — every quote from Coastline Solutions is free and obligation-free.
Call Blake on 0452 263 112 or send an enquiry through coastlinesolutions.com.au.

**Q: Do you do soft washing as well as pressure washing?**
A: Yes. We use soft washing on roofs, render and painted surfaces, and
high-pressure washing on driveways, pathways and bin pads — whichever is
safest and most effective.

**Q: Are you insured?**
A: Yes, fully insured. Happy to provide a certificate of currency before
any job starts.

**Q: Do you do regular lawn maintenance?**
A: Yes — weekly, fortnightly, monthly or one-off visits. Mowing, whipper
snipping, blowing, weeding and hedging.
